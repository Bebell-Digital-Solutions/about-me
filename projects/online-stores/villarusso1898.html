Problem & Protagonist

For over a century, Villa Russo 1898 had been synonymous with sophistication, a curated destination for fine tableware, jewelry, and perfumery. But by 2019, its legacy was at risk of fading in a fast-moving digital marketplace. The brand's charm lived within its physical store walls, yet online it had no voice.

Their website was static, unsearchable, and disconnected from the elegance of the in-store experience. The lack of e-commerce, SEO visibility, and modern design meant that younger audiences, the next generation of luxury buyers, never discovered them.

Aspiration & Agitation

The goal was bold: to modernize Villa Russo without losing its soul.

They wanted a digital experience that felt as curated as their store, one that could host a full online catalog, manage wedding registries, and present designer collections in an editorial, magazine-like format.

Every click needed to evoke craftsmanship, elegance, and exclusivity.

Bebell Digital Solutions didn't just build a store, we built a digital legacy.
