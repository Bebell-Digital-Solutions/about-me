Problem & Protagonist

HS Interior Design, led by Dis. Heydy Suárez, is known for transforming spaces into experiences. Yet behind the beauty of her work was growing chaos — hundreds of leads arriving from multiple platforms with no system to organize or prioritize them. Missed appointments and delayed responses became daily frustrations, consuming valuable creative time.

Aspiration & Agitation

Heydy’s goal was simple yet ambitious: to bring structure to her creative business. She wanted every inquiry to automatically lead to clarity — whether a free discovery call or a full design consultation. But without automation, she was stuck in manual follow-ups, losing valuable leads along the way.

Strategic Solution

Bebell Digital Solutions designed a bilingual digital funnel that connected interest to booking through clarity and automation.

Bilingual Landing Page

Developed in English and Spanish, this landing page guided visitors through Heydy’s services and seamlessly converted interest into booked calls.

Automated Booking System

We implemented smart scheduling that synced with Heydy’s calendar, categorized leads, and sent automatic confirmations and reminders.

Email Follow-up Sequences

We built nurturing flows that automatically responded to inquiries, shared resources, and guided prospects toward consultations — reducing manual tasks dramatically.

Challenges & Conquest

The Challenge: Creating automation that maintained HS Interior Design’s premium, personalized brand experience while simplifying lead flow.

Our Conquest: Through tailored design and automation, we balanced elegance and efficiency — giving Heydy a business system that reflects her artistry.
