Problem & Protagonist

Bebell Design Studio was managing client acquisition and project tracking manually. The process was time-consuming and limited their ability to scale architectural projects efficiently.

They needed a digital solution that could streamline client acquisition, project budgets, and execution workflows.

Aspiration & Agitation

The team wanted a system to automatically capture and manage clients, calculate budgets, and track project execution — freeing architects to focus on design and creativity.

Manual processes were slowing growth and limiting efficiency. The dream was a fully digital client and project workflow ecosystem.

Strategic Solution

Bebell Digital Solutions implemented a custom digital ecosystem for client acquisition and project management, integrating automation across all stages of the workflow.

Automated Client Acquisition

Capture, track, and nurture leads through a digital CRM tailored to architectural services.

Budget Calculation Automation

Integrated tools to automatically generate project budgets from client inquiries and scope details.

Project Workflow Optimization

Automated task tracking, deadlines, and resource allocation to improve project execution efficiency.

Challenges & Conquest

The Challenge: Digitizing years of client and project data while maintaining ongoing project delivery and workflow consistency.

Customizing automation to match architectural project management processes required precision and careful implementation.

Our Conquest:

We developed custom migration scripts, automated lead capture and workflow processes, and integrated all data into a CRM system without disrupting active projects.
