Problem & Protagonist

NEXED Travel, formerly known as ODTE, is the Dominican Republic’s leading cultural exchange agency. Despite their trusted reputation, their digital operations were falling short. The team managed registrations manually via email and WhatsApp, often losing potential applicants in the process.

Without CRM integration or lead tracking, they lacked visibility into marketing performance — and growth opportunities were slipping through the cracks.

Aspiration & Agitation

The NEXED team dreamed of a modern, user-friendly website where students could register for programs, receive automated updates, and be guided throughout their Work & Travel journey — all while reducing staff workload.

But without automation, ad tracking, or SEO, they couldn’t scale beyond manual processes or measure real campaign ROI.

Strategic Solution

Bebell Digital Solutions reimagined NEXED’s digital presence — not as a website, but as a bilingual recruitment system that integrates marketing, automation, and performance tracking.

Bilingual Web Platform

Developed a responsive website in English and Spanish, optimized for SEO and built to inspire trust among international audiences.

CRM Integration

Connected lead forms directly to NEXED’s CRM for instant data capture and segmentation, reducing manual entry time to zero.

Pixel Tracking & Ads

Installed Meta and Google ad pixels with conditional triggers to ensure accurate lead tracking and retargeting.

Localized SEO Strategy

Implemented dual-language SEO architecture using hreflang tags and localized keywords for top performance in both English and Spanish search results.

Challenges & Conquest

The Challenge: To unify CRM, bilingual SEO, and ad tracking within one system — without losing performance or data accuracy.

Our Conquest:

We created a middleware API to ensure real-time CRM sync, solved pixel duplication with server-side tracking, and built a scalable multilingual SEO foundation for future expansion.
